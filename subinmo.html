SubInmo

SubInmo is a 2D "flight simulator" based on gravity. The player pilots a space ship with limited fuel. Every boost counts, but the main way to move is by orbiting around an object in space and slingshotting your ship to the next neighboring celestial body. You’ll have to be careful not to boost into a planetary body or a black hole or you’re sure to lose!

For this project, I was Lead (and only) Programmer, and worked on the getting OpenGL alpha channels working, making specialized gravity physics, all gameplay and creating a set of defines for tweaking gameplay outside of the code.
