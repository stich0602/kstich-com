Tyrant

Tyrant is an MMO CCG with interactive battles! Gather your forces to defend against a massive onslaught from the traitorous Imperials, brutal Raiders, vile Bloodthirsty beasts zealous Righteous defenders, and the mysterious Xenos. Gather hundreds of cards and build a killer deck to take down other players!

Skyshard Heroes

Build up your hometown in the kingdom of Syrellia! Use your choice of Hero to command units and assault the enemy. Band together with other heroes to takeover conquest lands and possibly even the Bastion itself!

War Metal

War Metal is a Facebook RPG that puts you in charge as commander of a high-tech army. Build your force with devastating War Mechs, insatiable Bloodthirsty beasts, or a full-on Aerial squadron. Team up with hundreds of allies against massive Bosses and earn powerful rewards!

Play on Facebook »

Redshift

Redshift is a Sci-Fi MMORPG where you can explore the galaxy and battle players in real-time! Mine from asteroids, craft epic weapons, and build up the most powerful Battleship ever seen!
